Explore Pathanamthitta

Situated near the Western Ghats and bordered by the hills, Pathanamthitta district is a treat to eyes with its vast unending stretches of forests, rivers and rural landscapes. Blessed by nature, the district is famous for its scenic beauty, fairs and festivals. Learn more..

Sabarimala

One of the major religious hubs of South India, Sabarimala houses Sastha Temple situated about 80 km from Pathanamthitta town. Dedicated to Lord Ayyappa, this temple is situated in the lap of nature amid the dangerous hills of Western Ghats. Learn more..
